What we’re navigating

Parents of SENDbetweeners—the children who don’t fit the neat boxes, who are too complex for the system’s tick-boxes—are left to navigate a maze of disconnected services, each one passing the buck, each one failing to see the whole child. The law exists, the duties are clear, but the implementation fails. Parents are left alone, in crisis, with no manual.

This site is built by a neurodivergent parent, neuroscientist, and software engineer who learned the hard way and started building tools to help others: AI that knows the law and speaks to you like a human, resources that cut through the noise, and writing that says what most people are too exhausted to put into words.
